How to Make Any Birthday Special!

Instructable

What is needed

  • Decorating
    • Balloons
    • Birthday Banner
    • Wall Hanging Decoration
    • Tape
  • Cake
    • Cake Mix
    • Water
    • 2 Eggs
    • Cake Pan
    • Frosting
    • Candles
    • Cake Decorations
  • Presents
    • Birthday Card
    • Birthday Presents for your Friend
    • Wrapping Paper
    • Tape
  • Party
    • A Party Mood

Step 1: Get your Presents Ready!

  • Head to the store to grab your friend the perfect present
  • Don't forget to grab a birthday card as well
  • Grab some wrapping paper and tape
  • Wrap the present all cute
  • Set up an area specifically for gifts

Step 2: Make the Cake!

  • Grab your friend's favorite cake mix
  • Grab your friend's favorite frosting
  • Set the oven to the correct temperature
  • Make your cake batter
  • Pour into a cake pan
  • Bake the cake
  • Cool the cake overnight
  • Frost your cake
  • Decorate your cake
  • Put the candles in the cake
  • Snap a picture before it gets devoured

Step 3: Decorate for the Party!

  • Grab some balloons and other decor from the store
  • Call a friend to come help
  • Blow up all the Balloons
  • Hang up the wall decor first
  • Hang up the banner
  • Hang up some of the balloons
  • Disperse the rest of the balloons on the floor
  • Admire the beauty!

Step 4: Time to Party!

Check the list for the best places to eat dinner on your birthday in Missoula

  • Make reservations for your friend's favorite restaurant
  • Grab a group of friends and celebrate!
