Health Benefits

  • A greater amount of caffine and antioxidants than regular green tea.
  • Improved cardiovascular health and reduced risk of heart disease.
  • Cancer-fighting effects and protection against cellular damage.
  • Weight loss and improved metabiloc health.
  • Enhanced memory and cognative function

Ingredients

  • 1.5 teaspoons matcha powder of your choice
  • 1 tablespoon hot water
  • 2 teaspoons honey, or to taste
  • 3/4 cup hot milk or alternative milk of your choice
  • Your favorite mug
  • Small sifter
  • Whisk or electric milk frother

Step One

Gather the ingredients.

Step Two

Sift the matcha powder into the mug.

Step Three

Add the hot water and whisk until no lumps remian. Stir in the honey as well.

Step Four

Add the milk and either whisk to combine or use an electric milk frother; serve immediately.

Step Five

Enjoy your delicious and healthy drink!
